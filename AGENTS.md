## Caveman Guardrails

- Caveman skills are opt-in. Do not activate them unless the user explicitly asks for terse or caveman-style output.
- Clarity overrides terseness for security warnings, irreversible actions, onboarding explanations, and multi-step FFmpeg or audio-debugging procedures.
- Caveman skills complement the normal project guidance; they do not replace it.
